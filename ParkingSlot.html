STEPS ON HOW TO RESERVE YOUR PARKING SLOT

First, log in and fill out your name, email, and contact number before proceeding to view available slots for your selected month and days.

After logging in, you can view the available parking slots for your selected month and days. Simply click the arrow buttons to navigate between months and select “View Slots” to see the available days. Once you’ve chosen your desired slot, click the “Proceed” button to continue.
